DO's
Think for a minute before posting, especially when you’re angry or upset.
Keep your profile decent enough that you wouldn’t panic if a teacher or recruiter saw it.
Double-check news or information before sharing it.
Use privacy settings, but don’t fully rely on them.
Be respectful in comments, even during disagreements.
Clean up old posts once in a while.
DON'Ts
Don’t post personal fights or arguments online.
Don’t share private details like phone number, address, or college ID.
Don’t post impulsively just for attention or likes.
Don’t assume deleted posts are gone forever (screenshots exist).
Don’t troll or make fun of others—it can backfire.
Don’t compare your real life with others’ “highlight reels.”
