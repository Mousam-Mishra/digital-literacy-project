# Stay Safe from Ransomware – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Measures
- Do not download files or software from untrusted or unknown websites  
- Avoid opening suspicious email attachments or links  
- Use reliable antivirus software and keep it updated regularly  
- Keep your operating system and applications updated  
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Regularly back up important data to cloud storage or external drives  
- Avoid clicking on pop-ups or advertisements from unknown sources  

## UPI & Financial Safety
- Do not use public Wi-Fi networks while making UPI or online financial transactions  
- Always verify payment requests and recipient details before sending money  


## Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately:
- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Helpline Number:** 1930 (24x7)

## Note
Following these preventive measures can significantly reduce the risk of ransomware attacks and help protect personal and financial data.
